::Description
Description The 3'UTR of drosophila nanos mRNA contains the essential signals for generating the nanos gradient emanating from the posterior pole of drosophila embryos. The polarized distribution of nanos is generated by the localization dependent translation of nanos mRNA in the pole plasm.
