::Description
Description Alpha subunit of calcium calmodulin dependent kinase II (CaMKII) protein, highly enriched at synapse is important in synaptic plasticity, learning and long-term memory. The 5' part (nt 1-94) of 3'UTR of CaMKII mRNA mediates its dendritic targeting. An inhibitory element located downstream in the 3'UTR prevents CaMKII mRNA transport into dendrites.
