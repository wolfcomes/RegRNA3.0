::Description
Description The long half-life of alpha-globin mRNA (>24h in the developing erythroid cells) is mediated by three cytosine-rich regions, folded into a conserved secondary structure, located in its 3'UTR. These C-rich regions, also found in many other stable mRNAs, such as alpha(I) collagen, tyrosine hydrosylase, 15-lipoxygenase mRNAs and many others identified by microarray study(Waggoner and Liebhaber.
