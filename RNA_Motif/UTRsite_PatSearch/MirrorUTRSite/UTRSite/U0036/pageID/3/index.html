::Description
Description In the early embryo of drosophila several transcripts localize exclusively apically of the peripheral nuclei. Apical transport of hairy pair-rule mRNA is mediated by a 121 nt element located in the 3' untranslated region. This element comprises two stem-loop structures in which double-stranded stems have been observed to be essential for localization.
