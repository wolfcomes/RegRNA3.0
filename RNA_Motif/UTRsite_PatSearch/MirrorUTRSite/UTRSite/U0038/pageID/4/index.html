::Description
Description Protamine (Prm) mRNAs are translationally repressed for several days during male germ cell differentiation. Translational delay of Prm-1 mRNA involves the binding of Y-Box proteins MSY2 and MSY4 to a conserved region in the 3' most nt of the 3'UTR. The consensus site, denoted Y-Box Recognition Site (YRS) has been defined by mutational analysis and tested by the yeast three-hybrid system.
