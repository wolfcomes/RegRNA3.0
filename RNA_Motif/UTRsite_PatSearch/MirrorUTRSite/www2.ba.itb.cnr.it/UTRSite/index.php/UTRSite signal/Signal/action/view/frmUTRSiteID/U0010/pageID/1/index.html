::Description
Description AU-rich elements (AREs) present in the 3'untranslated region of mature lymphokine and cytokine mRNAs regulate mRNA stability and translational efficiency. Based on their sequence features and functional properties AREs can be divided into three classes. Class II AREs direct asynchronous cytoplasmic deadenylation (processive kinetics) generating poly-A(-) mRNAs.
