::Description
Description Upstream of N-ras (UNR) is a cytoplasmic protein containing five cold shock domains (CSD) (Jacquemin- Sablon et al., 1994). Proteins of the CSD family perform a variety of functions and are thought to behave as RNA chaperones, promoting a linear conformation of the mRNA.
