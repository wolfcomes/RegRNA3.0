Description Many Notch pathway target genes in Drosophila, including most members of the basic helix-loop-helix repressor family and the Bearded family, contain a series of conserved sequence motifs in their 3' UTRs, including the Brd box, the K box, and the GY box. The Brd box (AGCTTTA) is present in one or more copies in many of these 3'UTRs and mediates negative post-transcriptional regulation by affecting transcript stability and translational efficiency.
